Canada

General Information

Canada is the second-largest country in the world, known for its natural beauty and multicultural cities.

Major Cities

  • Toronto
  • Vancouver
  • Montreal
  • Calgary

Culture and Traditions

Canada's culture is influenced by its diverse population and historical ties to Britain and France.

Tourist Attractions

  • Banff National Park

Hong Kong

General Information

Hong Kong is a vibrant city known for its skyscrapers, harbour, and blend of Eastern and Western cultures.

Major Districts

  • Central
  • Kowloon
  • New Territories
  • Victoria Peak

Culture and Traditions

Hong Kong's culture is a unique mix of traditional Chinese practices and modern influences from its time as a British colony.

Tourist Attractions

  • Victoria Harbour
  • Hong Kong Disneyland
  • The Peak
  • Temple Street Night Market
